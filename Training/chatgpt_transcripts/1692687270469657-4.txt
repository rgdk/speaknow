Homeschooling is becoming popular in recent years as more and more people are becoming affluent and are able to afford being homeschooled. And another reason might be security concerns following the attacks on schools. There are several advantages and disadvantages. The advantages are having a one-to-one interaction with your teacher. But the disadvantage is the lack of social life from not having friends at school, just like normal people. And in my opinion, I wouldn't educate my own child at home.
